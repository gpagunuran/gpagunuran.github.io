About Siembra NC

Siembra NC is a grassroots organization dedicated to defending and recruiting community members for civic engagement and social justice initiatives. Through our volunteer programs and community meetings, we work to empower individuals and create lasting positive change in North Carolina.

Our meetings provide valuable information and opportunities for community members to get involved in meaningful work. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and experience levels.
