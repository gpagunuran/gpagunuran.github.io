Thank You for Registering!

What Happens Next?

After submitting this form:

  • Our volunteer coordinator will review your availability and interests
  • You'll receive a confirmation email within 2-3 business days
  • We'll schedule your orientation and first volunteer shift
  • You'll receive all necessary information, training materials, and resources

If you have immediate questions, please contact us at hola@siembranc.org.

Important: Once confirmed for a volunteer shift, please arrive 10-15 minutes early for check-in and any last-minute instructions. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please give us at least 24 hours notice when possible.
