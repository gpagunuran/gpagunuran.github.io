Pagun Design Firm

Pagun's Services

Pagun Design is a forward-thinking design firm that embodies the spirit of its name, which has diverse origins tied to structure and creativity. Drawing from the Sanskrit 'pagun', meaning "good or merit," and its ties to the word for the temple's foundation, the firm builds its work on the principles of integrity, reliability, and holistic management. This commitment extends to the firm's designs, which are informed by the Korean word for "pattern" or "design," 'p'agun', and the Filipino origin of "pagun", meaning to rise or ascend. By blending these linguistic roots into our main principles, Pagun Design crafts innovative and purposeful visual solutions that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also grounded in a deep sense of integrity and structural excellence.
